?

Used to match any letter anywhere in the word. More than one can be used.

Ex: ae? matches: ATE, EAT. Does not match ASK.

Ex: a?? matches: ATE. Does not match EVE.

"N"

Word must contain the letter(s) in quotes. Strings can be used, and multiples. So you can search for words containing "b" and "a", but "ba" looks for ba grouped together, not b and a separately. Duplicates are ignored; you can't check for more than one of a letter, unless they're grouped. "ss" will find KISS, but "s"ahp"s" will find both SAP and SASH

Ex: b"a"dder matches: BAD. Does not match BED.

Ex: "b"adde"r" matches: BARD. Does not match BEAD.

Ex: "br"adde matches: BRAD. Does not match BARD.

(N)

Word must contain the letter(s) at that exact location. NOTE: only counts by position from the beginning, not the end. You can't check for words ending with (N)

Ex: bs(a)tert" matches: BRAT. Does not match BREAST.

Ex: bs(at)ert matches: BRAT. Does not match BAT.

Ex: (b)satert" matches: BRAT. Does not match ABET.

Try some!

Any word containing s: be"s"tat

Any word containing es: b"es"tat

Any word with b as first letter: (b)estat

Any word with b as the third letter: be(s)at

Any word with b and a, and with st (grouped) as third and fourth letters: "b"e(st)"a"t
